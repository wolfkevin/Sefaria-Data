<EM><EN>Some hold that the Shira should be chanted aloud while

if one wishes to repent a serious sin, he should recite the Shira every 
day with great fervor. The <i>Zohar</i> says, ‘‘If one recites this 
song with elation and joy, he will be worthy to sing it in the next
