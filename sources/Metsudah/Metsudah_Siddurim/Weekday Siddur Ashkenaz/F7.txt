<M fnt>The first blessing of the <i>Birkas Hamazon</i> was composed by 
Moses in appreciation for the manna which God provided for the Jews in

<EM><EN>The second blessing, beginning with ‘‘Let us thank You,’’ was

Promised Land.<FL>¶
<EM><EN>The third blessing, beginning with ‘‘Have mercy,’’ was composed

<FL>¶
<EM><EN>The fourth blessing ‘‘Who is good and beneficent to all’’ was

burial.---<i>Maseches Berachos</i> 48b<FL>¶
